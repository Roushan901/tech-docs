# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is TechDocs?
TechDocs is a comprehensive learning platform for technical documentation, cloud computing, and DevOps practices. We provide guides, best practices, and real-world strategies for engineers at all levels.

### Who can use TechDocs?
Anyone! Whether you're a software developer, technical writer, DevOps engineer, or technical lead, you'll find valuable content tailored to your role.

### Is TechDocs free?
Currently, all content is freely available. We're transitioning to a premium membership model with advanced features, exclusive content, and community access.

### Do I need any prerequisites?
No! Our guides are designed for all skill levels. Each guide includes difficulty levels and prerequisites, so you can choose what's right for you.

## Content Questions

### How often is content updated?
We update guides regularly to reflect industry best practices and latest technologies. Major updates are announced via our newsletter.

### Can I download the guides as PDFs?
Yes! Most guides include a PDF download option. Premium members get access to complete course materials.

### Are the guides practical or theoretical?
Both! Every guide includes real-world examples, code samples, and actionable advice you can apply immediately.

### What topics are covered?
- Technical Writing & Documentation Standards
- API Documentation & Integration Guides
- Cloud Computing (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- DevOps & CI/CD Practices
- Infrastructure as Code
- Best Practices & Industry Standards

## Learning & Progress

### What are Learning Paths?
Learning Paths are curated sequences of guides designed to take you from beginner to expert in a specific area. They include recommended reading order and learning outcomes.

### How long do guides typically take?
Each guide includes an estimated reading/completion time. Most range from 10 to 45 minutes.

### Can I track my progress?
Premium members get access to progress tracking, bookmarks, and personalized learning dashboards.

### Are there certificates upon completion?
Yes! Upon completing a learning path, you'll receive a shareable certificate of completion.

## Technical Questions

### What technology stack is TechDocs built with?
TechDocs is built with Docusaurus v3, React, and modern web technologies. It's open-source and hosted on GitHub.

### Can I use content from TechDocs for my company?
Most content is available under a Creative Commons license. Premium members get additional usage rights for team training.

### How is my data handled?
We respect your privacy. No data is collected unless you explicitly subscribe to our newsletter. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

### Is TechDocs accessible?
Yes! We follow WCAG 2.1 guidelines to ensure accessibility for all users, including those with disabilities.
